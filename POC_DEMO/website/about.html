Who is it for?

Designed for adults with Retinitis Pigmentosa and related peripheral field loss conditions ‚Äî not fully blind, but unable to see hazards from the side.

üë• Primary Users

Adults with Retinitis Pigmentosa

Progressive peripheral vision loss (tunnel vision) affecting 1 in 4,000-5,000 people. They retain central vision but miss lateral hazards ‚Äî making audio-first guidance essential.

üéØ Core Priority
